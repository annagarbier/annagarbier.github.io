Light-activated sound

A photoresistor picks up ambient light signals and sends them to Arduino. Depending on the brightness of the light, a Processing sketch plays or pauses audio. For exsample, turn on a room's lights, and audio plays. Turn off the lights, and audio pauses.

Hardware: Photoresistor (input), breadboard, computer and speakers.
Code: arduino_hw_04.pde.

Hardware setup includes a single photoresistor as analog input for Arduino
