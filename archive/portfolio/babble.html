Babble

Prototypes and testing

While many speech synthesis technologies focus on the transformation of text into speech via a completely digital pipeline, few engage directly with the anatomical origins of vocalization. Further, few explore speech as a form of play that can exist beyond task-based scenarios.

Babble is a toy for playful speech synthesis: squeeze its nose, squish its face, and hear the differences in its synthesized voice.
