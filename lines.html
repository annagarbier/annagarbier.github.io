Moving lines

In 2011, I drew five drawings every day, each taking no more than a minute. Working at that pace, I found myself drawing sequences: watching the lines on my pages move, disappear, reappear, turn into recognizable objects, and then back into marks.

"Moving Lines" builds on that exploration of lines as transient marks, sometimes obviously connected to an artistâ€™s hand, and sometimes (seemingly) with an identity and direction of their own.
