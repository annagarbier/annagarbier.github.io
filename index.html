About

Originally trained as a linguist, I am currently pursuing an MFA in Design and Technology at Parsons School of Design. I am interested in language, communication, the patterns of nature, and the beauty of code. Before Parsons, I worked on Natural Language Processing and data policy at Google.

This site documents my work at (and beyond) Parsons.
