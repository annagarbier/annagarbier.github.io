Hindsight Guestbook

In the Spring of 2020, gallery spaces across the world were closed, classes finished online, and our MFA thesis festival– once meant to be a physical show– went completely virtual.

As part of our online exhibition (Hindsight 2020), we created a participatory Guestbook, a final last grasp for togetherness during the intesely digital time.

Every visitor to the festival website leaves anonymous data footprints. We use these footprints to build a story: one of people coming together from distant locations to share in learning, design, and art-making.
