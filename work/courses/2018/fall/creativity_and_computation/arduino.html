HW 04: Lights...and sound!

A photoresistor picks up ambient light signals and sends them to Arduino. Depending on the brightness of the light, a Processing sketch plays or pauses audio.

Turn on the room's lights, audio plays. Turn off the lights, audio pauses.

Hardware: Photoresistor (input), breadboard

Code: arduino_hw_04.pde

Hardware setup includes a single photoresistor as input for Arduino

HW 03: Shy Robot

An ultrasonic range finder reads the distance between the Arduino hardware and its environment. Based on the measured distance, a liquid crystal display (LCD) shows messages. Get too close to the sensor, and the buzzer also kicks in.

Hardware: Ultrasonic range finder (input), Liquid crystal display (output), Buzzer (output), Breadboard, Arduino, paper

Code: arduino_hw_03.pde
